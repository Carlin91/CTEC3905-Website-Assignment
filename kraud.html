Saud Kruger

Saud Kruger is a manufacturer of ships and equipment in the galaxy based in Bedaho. They primarily specialize in producing passenger vessels. Ships produced by Saud Kruger emphasize form over function for the sake of luxury, with designs incorporating tapering curves and large, tinted windows. Their default paint scheme is glossy white. Ships manufactured by Saud Kruger can exclusively install luxury Passenger Cabin suites
